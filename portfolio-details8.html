Abstract

For Startup Studio at Cornell Tech, my team is designing a generative AI fashion software, named Veloda, that assists independent fashion designers in their prototyping process. The software uses a moodboard, inspiration, future trends, and previous designs/garments made to output patterns to create a new design. Normally this process of prototyping is extremely labor intensive and by using this software we help foster creativity and simplification of prototyping since everything would be centralized. In essence, the software becomes a learning entity, adapting and evolving based on the vast dataset it processes.

For this project, I have prototyped the software's capabilities. By using AI in fashion design, the garments envisioned are nothing short of extraordinary, sculptural, and avant-garde. The software prototypes ability to sift through vast design possibilities, interpret trends, and offer fresh perspectives contributes to the creation of garments will transcend the ordinary.

In particular for this project, I created a stained glass themed dress that paints and displays the story of technology and how woman have furthered it. I have been interested in stain-glassed theme work and find it so delicate and intricate. Using my designed storyboards, I am hoping that the software outputs a sculptural design that maintains the intricacy and detail I want to portray while offering patterns that I wouldn't have known how to create organically. In fact, there are already digital creators (ex: @alxndraai) that use Midjourney or other custom generative AI softwares to showcase fashion ahead of its time. However, actually creating the garments from those images is where my software comes in. My software bridges the gap by offering the tech pack with patterns and grading for making the art come to life.

By completing this project, I aim to not only fund the sewing, constructing, and embellishing of these visionary garments but also to enhance and refine the software.
